Urban Farms LA

Think farmers markets, locally-sourced grocers, and farm box deliveries are the freshest produce options for the modern urbanite? The folks at Urban Farms LA are on a mission to bring local produce even closer to home. With custom garden boxes for your backyard, balcony, or even windowsill, farm-to-table is nearer than you think.
